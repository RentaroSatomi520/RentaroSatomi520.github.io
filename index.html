-Asleep World: terminalogy used to describe what happens in the world of the dead.

-Undeaths: People who attempt to escape Reapers, as well as death.
-Death Omens: People who have successfully escaped Reapers and cause havoc as well as distress to Reapers(Strong as well).
-Spectrals: People who have died but don't want to escape death, wants to pass on.
-Overseers: People who point Reapers to locations where someone has died, as well as manage Reapers. Not all Reapers have overseers. Overseers are typically veteran Reapers, but can be angels. Overseers work in colabration with Reapers in divisions.
-Reapers(slang:Reapes): People who make sure the dead, die, and hunt down those who escape death. ""Bad" People who have died, but his/her actions where "made up" and deem not so horrible that they have a choice, burn in the eternal pits of damnation, or be the Earth's personal janitor" - Pixel. Reapers who don't do their jobs, get hunted by angels, and sent to the void(basicailly killed).
-Asleep(applies to aniamls as well): People who are "dead" but don't fall in any of the other classification stated, typically live in Hell.
-Angels: People who were "good" in their lifetime and live in heaven. typically look down on the on all others.
-*Applies to all: eternal youth.
-Awake(applies to animals as well): Anything the qualifies as "living", and is unaware of anything happening in the "asleep world", nor can they see it.

-Hell: Where the Awake who are determined to be "wrong" go and become Asleeps.

No one knows that "already dead", go to the void, much like how humans don't know what happens when they die. Most assume the just disappear.
*The Undeaths souls that are "captured" by Reapers are sent to Hell, but if they are "killed" by a Reaper it goes to the Void.
